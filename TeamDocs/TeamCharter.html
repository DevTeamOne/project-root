Team Purpose / Mission Statement / Goals

 

Team Name: Team 1
Purpose: To collaborate, learn and achieve in the completion of assignments 1 and 2.
Mission Statement: To work effectively as a team environment to achieve our set goals.
Measuring Progress: Progress is monitored through the use of a Kanban board  (https://kanbanflow.com) to monitor work items.
How we make decisions:
  • We aim to be flexible.
  • All Ideas are welcomed. There are no silly ideas.
  • We adopt a "Yes and" approach rather than a "Yes But" approach to discussions.
  • We encourage a safe fail environment.
  • We brain storm ideas, We update Decision Register, We Do, We Peer Review.
  • ...And then we continuously challenge those ideas, to get the best of breed outcome. 
Expectations:
  • We work in an Agile fashion 
  • We have open, and honest communication,
  • All members have a voice.
  • We are active and engaged.
  • We have a Safe fail environment, and a no blame culture.
  • We will attend team meetings:
    • Daily SCRUM (Blog Entry)
    • Weekly Team Meeting will cover:
      • Product Review
      • Sprint Planning
      • Retrospective
  • We will own the SCRUM board and keep the task status up to date.

Operating Procedures (Communication Plan)

         Team Meetings

    1. Our team will endeveour to meet at a minimum of once per week for 1 hour (meeting, cadence & timeboxing)
    2. Agenda items for upcomming meetings can be added to the 'Team Meeting - Agenda / Minutes' Wiki page on the table provided
    3. Meetings will be conducted via Skype
    4. Responsibility will be shared amongst team members of who Chairs and who takes the Minutes for each meeting
    5. Any action items will be listed on the meeting Agenda and added to the Decision Register or KanBanFlow chart as necessary

         

         Communication

    1. Weekly meetings: Skype
    2. How we use Email:  Pop up on phone, Communications between meetings
    3. How we use Wiki: Working Guidelines
    4. How we use discussion board: Topic Based Discussions
    5. Team calendar: Critical events and dates
    6. Push mechanisms to communicate: KanBanFlow Board

         Decision Making

          As a team we will take the following approach when making decisions:

    1. Discuss any decisions as a group
    2. Update 'Decision Register' Wiki page
    3. Wait for each team member to cast their vote on the decision to be made
    4. Implement decisions based on majority vote
    5. Complete peer reviews
    6. Test Driven Design for code review

         Conflict

    1. Disagreements will be resolved by open communication and dialog between all team members
    2. If aggreements are not been met this will be discussed when we reflect in the team meeting Retrospectives agenda

         Reflection

    1. Retrospectives added as an agenda item for each team meeting
